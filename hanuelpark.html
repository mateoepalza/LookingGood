Hanuel Park

This park is situated in World Cup Park which was built to commemorate the 17th FIFA World Cup, situated in the highest part, you can enjoy an awesome and breathaking views of Seoul and Han River; the amazing landscapes are great to take awesome pictures and relaz in cozy rest areas.

Throughout all the year you can enjoy diferent festivals depending on the season, this autum in Hanuel Park or Sky Park you can enjoy the Seoul Eulalia Festival, held every year in October when the grass is in bloom, huge Miscanthus and sunflower fields.
