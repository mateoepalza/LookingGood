Seoul International Fireworks Festival (서울세계불꽃축제)

The Seoul International Fireworks Festival is held in early November of each year in front of the 63 City Building at Yeouido Hangang Park. This festival is one of Seoul's most popular fall events, and watching fireworks paint the sky and water in a breathtaking spectacle. The massive crowds that gather around the Hangang River also adds to the festive atmosphere.

For the best views, head over to Yeouinaru Station and walk along the river until 63 City Building. If crowds make you uncomfortable, go to the following locations to enjoy the fireworks in relative peace and quiet: Ichon Hangang Park, Nodeul Island, Hangang Bridge Observatory, Sayukshin Park, Seonyudo Park, and Noryangjin Seafood Market Parking Tower
