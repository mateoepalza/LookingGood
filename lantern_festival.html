Seoul Lantern Festival (서울빛초롱축제)

Like every year, the Cheonggyecheon Stream gonna be bright with the beautiful lanterns that you can see along 1.2 km from Cheonggye Plaza to Supyogyo Bridge.

The Seoul Lantern Festival started in 2009.The two week festival starts on Friday in the first week of November and covers downtown Seoul in sparkling lights, illuminating the city at night during the early winter.It combines both traditionalism and modernism through hundreds of thousands of lanterns.

Event

Lantern Festival

Location

Cheonggyecheon-ro, Seongdong-gu, Seoul

Date

Nov. 06, 2015 ~ Nov. 22, 2015

While last year's theme was the "Shining World Heritage of Seoul", this year's theme is "Illuminated Seoul Tour." Fall in love with Seoul's charm and friendliness at this festival!
