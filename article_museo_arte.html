Life is Beautiful

ARA Modern Art Museum

Mr brainwash is an street artist, his real name is Thierry Guetta, he's running an exhibition in Korea titled "Life is Beautiful"; it's a special exhibition to celebrate the opening of ARA Modern Art Museum.

During his visit to Korea, Mr. Brainwash met people from the Korean entertainment industry, including Yang Hyun-suk, CEO of Korea's leading record company YG Entertainment.
